Lasagna Bolognese

Lasagna bolognese is a layered style pasta with meat and cheese, originally from the Bologna region of Italy.

Ingredients:

  • 1 packet of oven-ready lasagna pasta
  • 1 jar of marinara sauce
  • Olive oil
  • 1 lb ground beef or pork
  • 1 small yellow onion, diced
  • 3 tbls butter
  • 3 tbls flour
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 2 cups of mozzarella, shredded

  1. Make the bolognese sauce. Heat the oil in the pan. Brown the meat. Add the marinara sauce to the meat and scrape the bottom of the pan.
